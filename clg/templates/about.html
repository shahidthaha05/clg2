About Us




Government Polytechnic College, Muttom

Empowering Future Engineers with Excellence in Education

Overview

Government Polytechnic College, Muttom (GPTC Muttom) is one of the premier institutions for technical education, located in the scenic region of Muttom, Kerala. The college offers a wide range of diploma courses in various branches of engineering and technology. With a focus on providing high-quality education, GPTC Muttom prepares students to excel in their respective fields and contribute effectively to the technological growth of society.

Mission & Vision

Mission

The mission of GPTC Muttom is to provide quality education and training in engineering and technology, foster innovation, and create skilled professionals who will contribute to societal and economic development. The college aims to be a center of excellence in technical education, producing graduates who are well-prepared to face the challenges of the ever-evolving global market.

Vision

Our vision is to empower students through education and skill development, enabling them to become responsible engineers and leaders in the field of technology. We strive to create a learning environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and ethical values, preparing our students to become highly competent professionals who contribute to nation-building.

History

Established in 1984, GPTC Muttom has been at the forefront of providing technical education to the youth of Kerala. Over the years, the college has built a strong reputation for academic excellence and infrastructure development. It was founded with the aim of offering a well-rounded education that equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for a successful career in engineering and technology.

Academic Excellence

GPTC Muttom is committed to providing students with a strong academic foundation. The college offers diploma programs in various disciplines of engineering including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, and Computer Engineering. The curriculum is designed to meet industry standards and incorporates practical learning, project work, and internships to enhance students' skills. The faculty comprises experienced professionals who are dedicated to guiding students toward academic success and professional growth.

In addition to the regular curriculum, the college provides various training programs, workshops, and seminars to further enrich students' learning experiences. The emphasis on hands-on training and industry exposure ensures that graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of the workforce.
