About GPTC Muttom

Government Polytechnic College, Muttom (GPTC Muttom) is a premier institution in the field of technical education, offering diploma courses in various engineering and technology disciplines. Established with the vision of creating skilled professionals, GPTC Muttom is dedicated to providing quality education that meets the needs of the industry and society. The college offers a vibrant learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty to nurture young talents and shape them into competent professionals.

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of diploma programs across various branches of engineering and technology:

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering
  • Diploma in Civil Engineering
  • Diploma in Computer Engineering
  • Diploma in Electronics Engineering

Facilities at GPTC Muttom

We offer state-of-the-art facilities to ensure an effective learning experience:

  • Well-equipped Laboratories: Modern labs for all engineering branches with the latest equipment and tools.
  • Library: A well-stocked library with a wide range of books, journals, and online resources.
  • Sports and Recreation: A variety of outdoor and indoor sports facilities to promote physical fitness and team spirit.
  • Hostel Accommodation: Comfortable and secure hostel facilities for both male and female students.
  • Computer and Internet Facilities: High-speed internet and computer labs for research, learning, and project work.
